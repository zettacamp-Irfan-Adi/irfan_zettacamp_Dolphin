Iron Man

Debut Date : March 1963

Superpower :

  • Powered armor: Iron Man's suit of armor gives him superhuman strength, durability, and the ability to fly.
  • Repulsor rays: Iron Man's hands and feet are equipped with repulsor rays, which can be used to blast enemies or propel him through the air.
  • Missiles: Iron Man's armor is equipped with a variety of missiles, which can be used to attack enemies from a distance.
